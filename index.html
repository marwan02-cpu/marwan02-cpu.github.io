Research: LLMs at the edge

★ Awarded the highest thesis mark in my school and cohort

Power Consumption of Operating Vision-Language Models on Edge AI Systems.

Problem

Vision-Language Models are compute-hungry and draw significant power, so IoT developers running them on the edge need to know their workload’s draw to size the right battery.

→

Solution

I built a framework from scratch that profiles a model’s power consumption on the edge, designed to generalize to any model, inference engine, and device.
